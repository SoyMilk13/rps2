How to Play?

In Rock Paper Scissors Two, there are 15 actions you can take. They are:

Rock, Fire, Scissors, Snake, Human, Tree, Wolf, Sponge, Paper, Air, Water, Dragon, Devil, Lightning, and Gun.

Attached above is a diagram demonstrating the interactions between all available choices. In short, each action defeats its following seven actions, and is defeated by its preceding seven actions.
